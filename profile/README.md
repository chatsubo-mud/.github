# Welcome to Chatsubo MUD

Welcome to Chatsubo MUD, a cyberpunk text-based multiplayer game set in the atmospheric world of William Gibson's Sprawl trilogy. Step into the gritty streets of Chiba City, where high tech meets low life, and the legendary Chatsubo bar serves as your gateway to adventure.

## About the Project

Chatsubo MUD brings to life the dark future of Gibson's universe through:

- **Rich storytelling and complex character interactions**
- **Immersive cyberpunk environment**
- **Dynamic quest system centered around the iconic Chatsubo bar**
- **Complex character progression with multiple roles and abilities**
- **Interactive world with roaming NPCs and challenging encounters**
